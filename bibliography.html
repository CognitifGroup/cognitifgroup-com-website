Bibliography.

Semantic Bathymetry™ draws upon established research in implicit cognition, psychometrics, semantic differential theory, response-time modelling and affective psychology. The publications below represent a selection of the academic literature that informs the design philosophy of the platform. They should not be interpreted as endorsements of, or direct descriptions of, the proprietary methods developed by Cognitif Group Limited.
